About SR Matrix Engineering

A Mumbai-based partnership built on stock availability, right-spec guidance, and long-term B2B relationships.

Who We Are

A dealer that speaks the furniture line's language

SR Matrix Engineering is a Mumbai-based supplier and dealer of industrial and furniture-grade gas springs (gas struts), serving furniture manufacturers, modular kitchen fabricators, and OEM assembly units across Mumbai and Navi Mumbai.

We supply gas springs for cabinets, storage beds, recliners, sofas and industrial lift applications — backed by prompt delivery, competitive pricing, and technical support on force selection and mounting configuration.

As a growing partnership firm, we focus on reliable stock availability and long-term B2B relationships with furniture and interior fit-out businesses, rather than one-off transactional sales.
